FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order? ▼

We recommend ordering at least 2-3 days in advance for custom designs. For simple orders, same-day delivery may be available. Reach out via WhatsApp for urgent requests!

Do you deliver outside Uthangarai? ▼

Currently, we deliver within Uthangarai and nearby areas. For distant locations, we can arrange pickup. Contact us to discuss delivery options!

Are your cakes suitable for allergies? ▼

Yes! We offer eggless, sugar-free, and allergen-friendly options. Please inform us about allergies when ordering so we can accommodate your needs.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, and digital payments via UPI. Payment options can be discussed when you place your order.
